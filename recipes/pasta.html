How to Make Pasta

Italian Pasta

This staple Italian cuisine is commonly enjoyed by thousands everday. Pasta is a culinary classic and is relatively simple to make.

Ingredients List

  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Salt
  • Butter

Preperation Steps

  1. Cook pasta in salted boiling water
  2. Warm tomato sauce and add beef
  3. Combine ingredients
  4. Add butter and salte to taste, then serve
