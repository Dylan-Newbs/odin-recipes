How to Make Tea

Green Tea

This staple beverage is commonly enjoyed by thousands everday. Tea is a timeless beverage which not only has culinary importance but cultural importance too.

Ingredients List

  • Water
  • Tea leaves or a Tea bag
  • Optional: Sweetener
  • Optional: Milk or Cream

Preperation Steps

  1. Heat water to near boiling
  2. Add Tea leaves or bag
  3. Allow to steep for roughly 3 minutes
  4. Add sweetener or cream to peronsal preference
